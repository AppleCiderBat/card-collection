Leafeon

Leafeon, the Verdant pokemon, gets its nutrition from photosynthesis. It lives a quiet life deep in forests where clean rivers flow.

  • Type: Grass
  • Height: 3′03″ (1.0 m)
  • Weight: 56.2 lbs (25.5 kg)

Leafeon is strong against Water, Ground, and Rock types. However, they are weak to Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, and Bug types.


Sandslash

Known as the Mouse pokemon, Sandslash curls up into a spiny ball when threatened. It can roll while curled up to attack or escape.

  • Type: Ground
  • Height: 3′03″ (1.0 m)
  • Weight: 65.0 lbs (29.5 kg)

Being a Ground type, Sandslash is strong against Fire, Electric, Poison, Rock, and Steel types. However, they are weak to Water, Grass, and Ice types.


Mightyena

Mightyena, the Bite pokemon, travel and act as a pack in the wild. They defeat foes with perfectly coordinated teamwork.

  • Type: Dark
  • Height: 3′03″ (1.0 m)
  • Weight: 81.6 lbs (37.0 kg)

Mightyena is strong against Psychic and Ghost types, while being weak to Fighting, Bug, and Fairy types.


Hariyama

The Arm Thrust pokemon. Although they enjoy comparing their strength, they’re also kind. They value etiquette, praising opponents they battle.

  • Type: Fighting
  • Height: 7′07″ (2.3 m)
  • Weight: 559.5 lbs (253.8 kg)

Hariyama is strong against Normal, Ice, Rock, Dark, Steel, and Bug types. They are weak to Flying, Psychic, and Fairy types.


Pachirisu

The EleSquirrel pokemon. It makes electricity with pouches in its cheeks and shoots charges from its tail. It lives atop trees.

  • Type: Electric
  • Height: 1′04″ (0.4 m)
  • Weight: 8.6 lbs (3.9 kg)

Pachirisu is strong against Water, Flying, and Steel types. However, they are weak to Ground types, and not very effective against Electric, Grass, and Dragon types.


Rapidash

The Fire Horse pokemon. Very competitive, this Pokémon will chase anything that moves fast in the hopes of racing it.

  • Type: Fire
  • Height: 5′07″ (1.7 m)
  • Weight: 209.4 lbs (95.0 kg)

Since it is a Fire type, Rapidash is strong against Grass, Ice, Bug, Steel, and Fairy types. It is, however, weak to Water, Ground, and Rock types.
